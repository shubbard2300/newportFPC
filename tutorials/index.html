1. How to Log In to WordPress

Visit newportfpc.org/wp-admin and enter your username and password. This is where you can access the WordPress dashboard to manage content on the website.

2. How to Edit a Post in WordPress

Once logged in, use the left-hand menu to go to Posts - select the one you want to edit, and click "Edit". Make changes and click "Update."

2. How to Edit a Page in WordPress

Once logged in, use the left-hand menu to go to "Pages" select the one you want to edit, and click "Edit". Make changes and click "Update."

3. How to Log In to Gmail

Go to mail.google.com, enter your email and password, and click "Next You'll then be taken to your inbox.

4. How to Compose and Send an Email

After logging in to Gmail, click the "Compose" button in the top left. Add the recipient's email, subject, and message. Click" "Send" when you're done.
