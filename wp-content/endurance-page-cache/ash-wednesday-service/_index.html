First Presbyterian Church of Newport, Oregon

Warmly welcomes everyone to join our worship and fellowship. We are a community rooted in hospitality, embracing all individuals through the inclusive vision of Jesus Christ, who calls each of us to gather in His love.

"There is no longer Jew nor Greek, there is no longer slave nor free, there is no longer male nor female; for all of you are one in Christ Jesus"
-(Galatians 3:28)
